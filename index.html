The signals are already there.
Your nurses just don't feel safe sharing them.

Every day, bedside nurses absorb what your culture produces: the moral injuries, the unspoken hierarchies, the moments that erode trust. These signals — the ones that predict turnover, safety events, and culture collapse — never reach leadership.

HeartVAULT™ creates a psychologically safe container where those signals can finally be named, captured, and translated into patterns you can act on.
